Welcome to Lochquarry Outdoor Centre

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre provides outdoor activities for young people aged 6-18. We have recently expanded the number of different activities that we offer and would like to invite you to explore the exciting adventures waiting for you.

Our Activities

The activities that the centre offers can be split into three main categories:

  • Land-based: Including Archery and Orienteering.
  • Water-based: Activities taking place on Lochquarry itself.
  • Rope-based: High-energy climbing and abseiling.

At the moment, the centre is busy during evenings and weekends with bookings from youth groups, such as Scouts and Guides, but we are expanding to include bookings from school groups during the week.
